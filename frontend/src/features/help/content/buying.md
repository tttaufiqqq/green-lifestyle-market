# Buying an Item
## Overview
Add items to your cart, choose how you'll receive each seller's items, and pay securely by FPX online banking.
## Steps
1. Find an item and tap **Add to Cart**.
2. Open your cart and tap **Proceed to Checkout**.
3. For each seller, choose **Meetup** (free) or **Shipping** (fee shown). Shipping needs your Malaysian address.
4. Tap **Pay**, choose your bank on the ToyyibPay page, and approve the payment.
5. You'll return to GLM and see your payment result and new orders.
## What happens next
Each seller confirms your order, then ships it or arranges the meetup. You'll get a notification at every step.
## Common issues
- If an item sells out while you're checking out → the page tells you which item changed; adjust and retry.
- If you closed the bank page without paying → your checkout expires after 30 minutes and stock is released. Just check out again.

⚠ Warning: Your payment is held safely by GLM and only released to the seller after you confirm you received the item.
