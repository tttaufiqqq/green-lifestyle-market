# Payments & Refunds
## Overview
Payments use FPX via ToyyibPay. GLM holds your money in escrow until your order completes.
## Cancelling
- Before paying: cancel anytime, nothing is charged.
- After paying but before the seller confirms: cancel and a full refund is arranged automatically.
- After the seller confirms: use **Request Refund** with a reason; an admin reviews it.
## Refund process
1. Submit the request on the order page.
2. Admin reviews (you'll be notified of approve/reject).
3. Approved refunds are transferred to your bank; the reference number appears on the order.
## Common issues
- Charged but order shows unpaid → verification can take a minute; if it persists, the team is alerted automatically and will resolve it.

⚠ Warning: GLM never asks for your banking password. Payment happens only on your bank's official FPX page.
