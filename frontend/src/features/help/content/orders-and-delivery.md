# Orders & Delivery
## Overview
Every order moves through clear statuses you can track on the order page: Paid → Confirmed → Shipped or Ready for Meetup → Completed.
## Steps (receiving)
1. Watch notifications for **Confirmed** and **Shipped/Meetup** updates.
2. For shipping, use the tracking number shown on the order.
3. For meetup, check the seller's note for time and place.
4. Once you have the item, tap **Confirm Receipt**.
## What happens next
Confirming receipt completes the order and releases payment to the seller. If you do nothing, orders auto-complete 7 days after shipping/meetup.
## Common issues
- Item hasn't arrived and it's been a while → contact the seller via the meetup note details, or **Request Refund** on the order.
- Wrong or damaged item → **Request Refund** with photos described in your reason.
