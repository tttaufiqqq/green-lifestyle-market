# FAQ
**Is GLM free to use?** Browsing and buying are free. Sellers pay a 5% fee on the item price (not on shipping).
**Who can sell?** Any verified user — UTeM students, staff, or the public.
**What can I sell?** Eco-friendly, sustainable, or pre-owned items in the listed categories. Admins may remove listings that don't fit.
**When do sellers get paid?** After the buyer confirms receipt (or 7 days after delivery), in the next payout run.
**Which payment methods work?** FPX online banking (all major Malaysian banks) via ToyyibPay.
**Can I pay cash at a meetup?** v1 requires online payment even for meetups — it's what protects both sides.
**How do I report a problem?** Use **Request Refund** on the order, or the Contact page for anything else.
