# Getting Started
## Overview
Green Lifestyle Market lets you buy and sell eco-friendly and pre-owned items within the UTeM community and beyond.
## Steps
1. Create an account with your email and choose your affiliation.
2. Open the verification email and click the link.
3. Browse items on the home page, or tap **Sell** to list your first item.
## What happens next
Once verified you can buy and sell. Unverified accounts can only browse.
## Common issues
- If the verification email doesn't arrive → check spam, then use **Resend verification** on the login page.
- If you're locked out after failed logins → wait 15 minutes and try again.

💡 Tip: Add your phone number so sellers can coordinate meetups faster.
