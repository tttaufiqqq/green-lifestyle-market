# Selling an Item
## Overview
List an item in minutes. GLM collects payment for you and pays you out after the buyer confirms receipt.
## Steps
1. Tap **Sell**, add 1–5 photos, title, category, condition, price (minimum RM1) and quantity.
2. Pick at least one fulfilment option: meetup (set a location) and/or shipping (set a flat fee).
3. Tap **Publish** (or **Save Draft** to finish later).
4. When an order comes in, tap **Confirm**, then **Mark Shipped** (with tracking) or **Ready for Meetup** (with a time and place).
## What happens next
After the buyer confirms receipt, the order shows under **Payouts → Eligible**. Admin transfers your earnings (item price minus 5% fee, plus your full shipping fee) to your bank account, usually weekly.
## Common issues
- If Publish is disabled → verify your email first.
- If your payout doesn't appear → check your bank account details are saved and verified under **Profile → Bank Account**.

📌 Note: Be honest with condition grading. Orders can be refunded if the item isn't as described.
